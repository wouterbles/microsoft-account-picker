# Privacy Policy

**Microsoft Account Auto-Picker** | *Last updated: December 6, 2025*

## Data Collection

This extension does **not** collect, transmit, or share any data with external servers or third parties.

## Local Storage

The extension stores your account preferences (service identifiers, selected email addresses, and enabled status) locally using your browser's `storage.sync` API. This data never leaves your browser except through your own browser sync.

You can view and delete all stored data anytime via the extension popup.
